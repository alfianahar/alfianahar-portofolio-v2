## Problem

Visitors need a faster way to understand the portfolio without reading every page manually.

## Solution

The assistant interface provides guided prompts and controlled answers around profile, work, services, and contact paths.

## Outcome

The feature frames AI as useful navigation instead of fake intelligence.
